LocalMind - Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Does LocalMind collect my data?

No. LocalMind does not collect any data. The app uses Apple Intelligence, which processes your requests according to Apple's privacy policies.

Where is my data processed?

Your requests are processed by Apple Intelligence, either on your device or using Apple's Private Cloud Compute servers. LocalMind does not have access to your data.

Do I need an internet connection?

Apple Intelligence may require internet for some requests when using Private Cloud Compute. However, many requests can be processed entirely on your device.

What are the system requirements?

Required:

  • macOS 15.0 (Sequoia) or later
  • Apple Silicon Mac (M1, M2, M3, or M4)
  • Apple Intelligence enabled in System Settings

Note: Intel-based Macs are not supported.

Why isn't LocalMind working?

Make sure:

  • You're running macOS 26.0 or later
  • Apple Intelligence is enabled in System Settings
  • Your Mac has an Apple Silicon chip (M1 or later)

Is LocalMind available in my country?

LocalMind is available where Apple Intelligence is supported. Check Apple's website for availability in your region.
