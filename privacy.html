Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

Introduction

LocalMind ("I," "my," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how LocalMind handles information when you use this macOS application.

The Short Version

LocalMind does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data. Period.

All AI processing happens entirely on your Mac using Apple's on-device foundation models. Your data never leaves your device.

Information Collection and Use

What I DON'T Collect

LocalMind does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • ❌ Text you input or generate

  • ❌ Code you analyze
  • ❌ Usage data or analytics
  • ❌ Crash reports or diagnostics
  • ❌ Device information
  • ❌ IP addresses
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Cookies or tracking data
  • ❌ Any other user data

How LocalMind Works

LocalMind operates entirely on-device:

  1. You type text into the application
  2. Processing occurs locally on your Mac using Apple's Core ML framework
  3. Results are displayed within the app
  4. Nothing is sent to external servers
  5. Nothing is stored beyond your current session (unless you manually save)

On-Device Processing

All features utilize Apple's on-device AI models:

  • Text Generation: Processed locally on your Mac
  • Email Drafting: Processed locally on your Mac
  • Code Analysis: Processed locally on your Mac
  • Streaming Responses: Generated locally on your Mac

Your content remains private and under your control at all times.
