Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Oct 19, 2025

LocalMind does not collect any data from you.

This app uses Apple Intelligence to process your requests. All processing is handled by Apple's systems according to Apple's privacy policies.

What Data LocalMind Collects

None. LocalMind does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

How LocalMind Works

When you use LocalMind:

  1. You enter text into the app
  2. The app sends your request to Apple Intelligence
  3. Apple Intelligence processes your request
  4. The response is shown in the app

LocalMind does not see, store, or transmit your data. Processing is handled entirely by Apple Intelligence.

Apple Intelligence

LocalMind uses Apple Intelligence, which is provided by Apple Inc.

Apple Intelligence may process requests:

  • On your device
  • Using Apple's servers (Private Cloud Compute)

Apple Intelligence is governed by Apple's privacy policies, not by this app.

Learn more: apple.com/privacy

What LocalMind Stores Locally

The app stores only basic settings on your Mac, such as:

  • Window size
  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Font size

These settings contain no personal information and are stored only on your device.

Third-Party Services

LocalMind uses only Apple Intelligence. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

LocalMind does not collect data from users of any age.
